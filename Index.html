ANY1 - Virtual PC

Aimed at providing anyone with a Virtual PC that has great specifications. The project is based on cloud technology and utilizes various Amazon Web Services (AWS) such as EC2, VPC, Load Balancer, and Auto Scaling. The project aims to enable users to easily access and use a Virtual PC that meets their specific needs
